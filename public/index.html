About Raha Voyage Safaris

At Raha Voyage Safaris, we are your premier partners in creating unforgettable adventures across the heart of Uganda. Our passion lies in revealing the breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cultural heritage that this beautiful country has to offer.

With a team of experienced guides and safari experts, we are committed to providing personalized, authentic experiences that go beyond your expectations. Whether you're seeking thrilling wildlife encounters in Uganda's national parks like Bwindi Impenetrable Forest or Murchison Falls, or you want to immerse yourself in the rich local culture, we tailor safaris to match your unique interests and preferences.

Our Commitment

Our dedication to responsible tourism and sustainability influences everything we do. We prioritize conserving Uganda’s natural resources and supporting local communities through our initiatives. When you choose Raha Voyage Safaris, you’re not only embarking on an extraordinary journey but also contributing to the well-being of the country and its people.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We provide genuine, immersive experiences that capture the true essence of Uganda.
  • Sustainability: Our responsible tourism practices protect the environment and uplift local communities.
  • Excellence: We deliver outstanding service, ensuring attention to detail in every aspect of your safari.
  • Passion: Driven by our deep love for Uganda and its wildlife, we share this passion with our clients.
  • Innovation: We constantly seek new and exciting ways to enhance your safari experience.

Queen Elizabeth National Park Safaris (3 Days Safari)

Uganda Gorilla and Wildlife Safari (7-Days)

The Uganda Gorilla and Wildlife Safari is a thrilling adventure that takes you deep into Bwindi Forest to encounter mountain gorillas up close, alongside exciting game drives, boat cruises, and more.

Murchison Falls Safari

Murchison Falls is a stunning natural wonder where the Nile River forces through a narrow gorge, creating a powerful waterfall amidst breathtaking landscapes and diverse wildlife.

Rwenzori Mountains Trekking

"Embark on a trek through the Rwenzori Mountains' lush forests and icy peaks, discovering breathtaking landscapes and diverse wildlife in Uganda's highlands."

Kidepo Valley National Park Safaris

Kidepo Valley National Park is a breathtaking wilderness with dramatic landscapes, rare wildlife like cheetahs and lions, and rich cultural encounters with the Karamojong and Ik tribes, offering an authentic and unforgettable safari experience!

book now

Sipi Falls Safaris

Sipi Falls is a stunning destination featuring a series of three majestic waterfalls cascading down the slopes of Mount Elgon. Nestled in eastern Uganda, this natural wonder offers breathtaking views, lush landscapes, and the opportunity for adventurous activities like hiking and canyoning. Visitors can explore the scenic beauty of the falls, engage with local communities, and experience a serene and memorable safari adventure.
